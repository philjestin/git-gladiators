⚡ POWER SCORE

Your Power Score combines a weighted base with three advanced modifiers:

Commits 40% log₁₀(commits + 1) × 100
Lines Added 35% log₁₀(additions + 1) × 10
Lines Deleted 25% log₁₀(deletions + 1) × 10

📐 Logarithmic scaling prevents outliers from dominating. 10× more commits ≠ 10× the score.

🔥 STREAK BONUS

Rewards consecutive weeks of activity (up to +15 pts):

Per Week +3 pts Each consecutive active week
Maximum +15 pts Capped at 5 weeks

🔥 Stay active — consistent weekly commits build your streak and boost your score.

📊 CONSISTENCY

Multiplies base score based on how evenly you contribute (0.85× to 1.15×):

Even Distribution 1.15× Same effort every week
Bursty Pattern 0.85× All work in one big spike

📊 Needs 3+ active weeks to kick in. Uses coefficient of variation to measure spread.

♻️ CHURN PENALTY

Penalizes write-then-rewrite patterns (0 to -10 pts):

Churn Detection -10 max High adds followed by high deletes
Clean Code 0 pts No churn detected

♻️ Needs 2+ active weeks. Detects when additions in one week get deleted the next.

✅ Commits matter most — they represent completed units of work

✅ Deletions are valued — cleanup and refactoring is important

✅ Log scale is fair — prevents LOC spam from dominating
